AUTHOR'S PAGE : About The Author
#ILoveSushi ! Hi there! I am Ingrid Dominique S. Sampang of 9-Arayat, the creator of this website. As you can probably tell by now, I am very passionate about my love for sushi. Personally, I find sushi as one of the most comforting things that can cheer me up on a bad day. It's a type of food that makes my heart feel warm when I eat it. I suppose this may be because of the sentimental memories I associate with sushi or simple because the Japanese delicacy is just THAT good.. Additionally, the fact that authentic sushi is expensive and limited in a sense that I cannot eat it everyday, when I do get the chance to indulge in its flavors, it really is something that I look forward to.
